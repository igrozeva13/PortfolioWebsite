A Collection of My Web Designs


WORK #1

Client: Essential StaffCARE

Skills: Responsive Design, HTML & CSS, jQuery


This is a landing page I created for my current employer, Essential StaffCARE. The purpose of this landing page is to link to our online enrollment form, which is created by a third party administrator. This landing page serves to inform employees about their Health Care options before signing up for coverage. Each landing page includes custom logos and links for each client. I developed the design and coded the page in HTML. The main challenge I faced with this landing page project was absorbing a lot of the responsibility that goes along with these pages. Previously, these landing pages were handled by an external marketing company who managed them for us. I wanted to redesign them, but I also wanted to bring the responsibility in house. To do that, I worked with a hosting company who helped me convert the page into Wordpress for easy management. A live example is available to view at www.esc-enrollment.com/spherion.

WORK #2

Client: Essential StaffCARE

Skills: HTML, CSS, jQuery


I designed and coded this webpage for Essential StaffCARE. This page serves as a guide to employees who are signing up for insurance through Essential StaffCARE and includes Frequently Asked Questions and descriptions of the plans available to employees. This information was originally in the enrollment form packet, but in an effort to push down printing costs and cut down on paper waste, this information was pushed online. The challenge in designing this page was to take a lot of content and make it succinct and understandable. I wanted this page to look friendly and inviting and break down all the content into managable groups so employees can quickly find the information they seek. Coding this page provided me with more experience in jQuery and allowed me to explore adding interactivity to a page. A live example is available to view on my github site for now, www.igrozeva13.github.io/FAQ., while the page content is being vetted by ESC's underwriter and administrator.
