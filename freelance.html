FREELANCE

A Collection of My Freelance Design Works


WORK #1

Client: Plumbing by Paul

Skills: Logo Design


This is an example of a logo I designed for a local start-up plumbing company. This project was fun because I was given complete creative freedom. I deliberatey chose each element and I was able to create a modern, easy to recognize logo for my client. I also printed the logo as a sticker for the company's work truck and created a few promotional pieces.

WORK #2

Client: Greenville Lawn Care Service

Skills: Vector Art, Layout and Design


I currently work with a local company in Greenville, and I have created a variety of promotional work, including lawn signs, flyers and postcards. The main challenge I face with these projects are tight deadlines. The company usually needs a quick turnaround so my ability to work quickly and efficiently is valued. The client usually gives me a lot of freedom with design, and I chose the color scheme, the art and the typography. I have come up with a consistent look for all pieces that I create in an effort to establish a brand identity.

WORK #3

Client: Justine and Nate O'Leary

Skills: Typography and Design


This is a wedding invitation I created and had printed for a couple of friends. I was given the theme and colors of the wedding and was asked to design an invite. I came up with an idea to have the Invite, RSVP card, and rehearsal dinner layered to look simple and cohesive. The challenge was aligning the graphics so they would print properly and look clean when stacked on top of each other. The other challenge was selecting colors that would print correctly on the paper that I chose. I worked closely with a print vendor to select the paper and prepare the invites.
