About Me

Hello, my name is Ina Grozeva. I’m a Bulgarian born, American raised Graphic Designer looking to make the world a better place. I think the power of good design is sometimes undervalued but I believe that we have the ability and responsibility to inform, educate, and bring awareness to the masses about the world around them. Great design also just makes the world shine a little brighter. When I’m not on a computer, I enjoy hanging out with my cat, reading a good book, cooking, and being outside in the world. I’m an avid Game of Thrones watcher and Harry Potter nerd. Reach out to me and let’s work together!
