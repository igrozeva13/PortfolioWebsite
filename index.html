ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Ina Grozeva. I’m a Bulgarian born, American raised Graphic Designer looking to make the world a better place. I think the power of good design is sometimes undervalued but I believe that as designers we have the ability and responsibility to inform, educate, and bring awareness to the masses. Great design also just makes the world shine a little brighter. I have a degree in Visual Communications from Ohio University and I am a elf-taught web designer. I have a passion for learning and I am always trying to improve my design skills.

When I’m not at my computer, I enjoy hanging out with my cat, reading a good book, cooking, and doing outdoor activities. I’m an avid Game of Thrones watcher and Harry Potter nerd. Reach out to me and let’s work together!
